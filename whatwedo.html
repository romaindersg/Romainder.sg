What We Do

ECObites: A BAKESALE FOR THE EARTH

Our inaugural initiative focuses on circularity. All funds raised are directed to the ROMAINDER fund, supporting upcycling efforts, education, and sustainable events.

❮ ❯

Beach Cleanups: Youth in Action

ROMAINDER organizes beach cleanups, connecting youth nation-wide for a common goal. In 2023, we raised over $12,000 and diverted 100KG+ of recyclables.

❮ ❯

RED CROSS INTERNATIONAL FAIR

Our team managed to upcycle 600+ books and stationeries, ensuring nothing goes to waste and resources are repurposed for the community.

❮ ❯

Global Student Empowerment Workshops

Beyond local events, ROMAINDER facilitates workshops empowering students worldwide, equipping the next generation with the tools to build a circular economy.
